# GEOL 593 / ATMS 597: SIMLES - Statistical Inference and Machine Learning for Earth and Environmental Sciences 

This is a live, and very much beta version of the course notes for the GEOL 593/ATMS597 class  as it is being currently taught in the Fall of 2021 at the University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign. The course is intended as a graduate-level hands-on introduction to Statistical Inference and Machine Learning methods  across Earth and Environmental Sciences.  

:::{Warning}
The book is actively being changed and updated. Nothing is stable. If you are not a student in the class, proceed with cautions. Here be dragons.
:::

To run a notebook in this book use the launch button (the little rocket at the top of notebooks) to launch either Binder Hub or Google Colab.
